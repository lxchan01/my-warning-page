Schedule X - Drugs which are habit forming, psychotropic and other drugs likely to be misused for addictive purposes.

Falsely claiming to be a registered pharmacist

can result in a fine of up to Rs.500 for the first offense and up to six months of imprisonment or a fine up to Rs.1000, or both, for subsequent offenses.

Dispensing by unregistered persons:

Dispensing by unregistered persons is punishable with imprisonment up to 6 months, or with fine up to Rs 1,000 or with both.
